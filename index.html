QUESTIONS

What if I don't have Starlink?

You don't need it. Your phone relays messages for others. As long as someone in your mesh has internet access, everyone can communicate. You're a bridge, not a dead end.

How far does Bluetooth reach?

About 100 meters in open space, less through walls. But that's the point ‚Äî messages hop through many phones. You only need to reach your neighbor.

Can the government detect it?

Bluetooth signals are everywhere ‚Äî headphones, speakers, watches. The app looks like noise. And even if they find it, the messages are encrypted and the network has no center to attack.

What about my safety?

The panic button wipes everything instantly. After deletion, the app looks freshly installed. We can't protect you from everything, but we've designed for the worst case.
